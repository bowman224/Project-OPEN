About Project OPEN

Project OPEN connects people to the local community and the open resources of San Francisco. The places on the Project OPEN map share the following values:

  • Open access: open to public, free, accessible
  • Open-minded: welcoming to all
  • Open books: transparency into operations, funding, etc.
  • Open source: making the info available to other orgs
